Choclolate reciepe

Today we are covering how to make a chocolate cake

ingredients

  • 220g unsalted butter,softened
  • 220g (7 3/4oz) caster sugar
  • 220g self-rasing flour
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanila extract
  • 3 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • small pinch salt

Cooking

This will take you approximatly 25 mins to cook
You shuld also take 20 mins to prep

  1. Step 1
    • pre-heat the oven on fan
    • Heat up butter untill melted
  2. Step 2
    • into bowl add:
        1 cup of sugar
        2 whole eggs
        1 tea spoon of vanila extract
  3. Step 3
      Into another bowl add:
      • Half cup flower
      • 1/3 cup of cocoa
      • Tea spoon of backing powder
      • Half tea spoon of salt
  4. Step 4
    • Now combine both bowls mad in step 2 & 3
    • Along with the melted butter and mix !
  5. Step 5
    • Now place the mixing into a backing tray
    • Cook for 25 mins until soft and gooey inside
  6. Step 6
    • BONE A APETTE
    7. Choclolate cake
